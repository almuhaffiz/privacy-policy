Privacy Policy for Al Muhaffiz

Effective Date: July 13, 2025

Introduction

Al Muhaffiz is a mobile application designed to assist users in studying and memorizing the Holy Quran through various memorization methods. This Privacy Policy outlines how Al Muhaffiz ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you use our application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled responsibly.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use Al Muhaffiz:

1. Personal Information

  • User-Provided Information: When you register or interact with certain features of the App, you may voluntarily provide personal information such as your name, email address, or other contact details.
  • Progress Data: The App may collect data related to your memorization progress, such as completed chapters, verses, or study sessions, to provide personalized feedback and track your learning journey.

2. Non-Personal Information

  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymized data about how you interact with the App, such as the features you use, time spent on the App, and preferences for memorization methods.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about your device, including device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and network information, to optimize the App's performance.

3. No Sensitive Personal Data

Al Muhaffiz does not collect sensitive personal data such as biometric data, financial information, or precise location data unless explicitly required and consented to for specific features.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve the App’s functionality, such as tracking your memorization progress and offering personalized study plans.
  • To respond to user inquiries, provide customer support, and send important updates about the App.
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve the App’s performance, user experience, and content.
  • To ensure the security and integrity of the App.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the App, such as cloud hosting or analytics providers, under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect the rights, safety, or property of Al Muhaffiz, our users, or the public.
  • Aggregated Data: We may share anonymized, aggregated data for research or statistical purposes, which cannot be used to identify you.

Data Storage and Security

  • Storage: Your data is stored securely on our servers or those of our trusted service providers. Personal information and progress data are stored for as long as necessary to provide the App’s services or as required by law.
  • Security: We implement industry-standard security measures, such as encryption and access controls, to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices and Rights

  • Access and Control: You may access, update, or delete your personal information by contacting us through the App or at the email provided below.
  • Opt-Out: You may opt out of receiving non-essential communications from us by following the unsubscribe instructions in those communications.
  • Data Portability: You may request a copy of your progress data in a structured, commonly used format.
Third-Party Links and Services

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services, such as Quran resources or external study tools. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before interacting with them.
